About us

Corbel is an artificial intelligence chat assistant designed to assist users with a variety of tasks and inquiries. Whether you need help with everyday tasks, information about a particular topic, or just someone to talk to, Corbel is here to help. With its advanced natural language processing capabilities, Corbel is able to understand and respond to a wide range of requests and questions, making it a convenient and valuable tool for both personal and professional use. Whether you're looking for assistance with scheduling, research, or simply need someone to listen, Corbel is ready to assist you.
